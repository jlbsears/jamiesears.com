About Me



Welcome to my "about me" page! I am a Senior at Lindenwood University pursuing my Bachelors degree in Interactive Media and Web Design. I am set to graduate in December of this year. When I graduate I hope to get a job at a St. Louis based firm to pursue a career in web design and development.

I got married in August of 2012, to the best guy in the world! He is a software engineer and it is because of him that I revealed my inner "geek." When we met I was working towards a degree in education so I could teach High School Math. When we got engaged I designed our wedding invatations and made us a wedding website. It was then that I realized my love for front end development.

When I am not sitting at a computer designing I love being outdoors, doing things such as playing volleyball, softball, riding four wheelers, and hunting.
Thanks for taking the time to get to know me a little bit.

Jamie
